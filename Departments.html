DEPARTMENT OF ENGLISH

WELCOME TO MOUNT SEENA COLLEGE OF ARTS & SCIENCE

The department of English started its functioning from 2010 .With a vision of creating good citizens with ample communicative skills in English ,the department offers core courses and common courses to all departments of the college –BA / BSc / BCom / BBA .The department conducts various literary fests to assess and evaluate the advancement of the students and to motivate them. The students put their heads and hearts together to participate actively in all the curricular, extra curricular and extension activities.

Vision

With the Vision of Imparting Education to the Students, the magnanimous Management of our College annexed an exclusive Section in Commerce (UG AND PG) under the Self-Financing Stream in the Academic Year 2022-2023, in addition to the Aided section. Develop students into the centre of excellence in Commerce and Management field and help them for economic self sufficiency.

Mission

Give Commerce and Management Education to make the students capable of applying the learned principles in real life practical situation and make them to become entrepreneurs and business leaders.

Quality Policy

The Department of Commerce is one of the Prestigious Departments of the College and B.Com is one of the most Demanded Courses of the contemporary academic world. Ever since its inception, the Department has recorded consistent improvement in its Academic Performance Give excellent Commerce and Management education to the students and instill in them respect and appreciation in the field of Management through innovative and value oriented education.
